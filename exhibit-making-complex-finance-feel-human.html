Title Wall

How do we get 1,000,000 people creating on the Nothing Phone 3?

Nothing | 2026 | D&AD

Context

Nothing has built a strong design-led identity, but reaching a million creators requires the phone to become a tool people actively make with, not just admire.

Key Research

Use this section for research on creator habits, mobile-first making, social expression, and what makes a device feel like part of someone's creative identity.

Insight

People do not become creators because a tool is powerful. They become creators when a tool makes expression feel immediate, visible, and worth sharing.

Idea

Position Phone 3 as a creative catalyst, building a campaign that turns everyday users into visible makers and makes creation itself feel like the product story.

Execution

Study 01

Platform ideas that invite people to create, remix, and publish directly from the device.

Study 02

Messaging that reframes specs as creative possibilities instead of engineering brag points.

Study 03

Creator partnerships and challenges designed to scale identity, not just awareness.

Reflection

This exhibit is really about turning tech enthusiasm into creative momentum people can see themselves inside.
