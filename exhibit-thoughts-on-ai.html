Title Wall

How can I use AI to improve my work without losing my creativity?

Bryce Peterson | 2026 | Thought Piece

Context

AI is becoming part of my process whether I invite it in or not. The real question is not whether to use it, but how to use it in a way that sharpens my thinking instead of flattening it.

Key Research

Add the books, interviews, workflow experiments, and creative examples that helped shape your point of view on AI as a tool rather than a replacement.

Insight

AI is most useful when it expands the room around an idea, not when it decides what the idea should be.

Idea

Frame this piece as a personal philosophy of use: where AI helps with speed, pattern recognition, and iteration, and where human taste, judgment, and spirit still have to lead.

Execution

Study 01

Examples of where AI helps me widen research and surface new connections faster.

Study 02

Moments in the process where human taste and authorship have to take over.

Study 03

A practical workflow for using AI as a collaborator without outsourcing creative conviction.

Reflection

This exhibit is less about having a final answer and more about building a disciplined way to stay creative, curious, and unmistakably human while working with new tools.
